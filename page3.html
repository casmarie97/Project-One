Relaxing Activities

There are a lot of things that come to mind when you think of a lazy afternoon activity. Reading books is intellectually engaging but you can do it in comfortable positions at home, on your bed, the couch, and most importantly in your PJs.

Another activity to relax the body is yoga. Focusing on the breath and the way your body feels helps you stay in the present moment. Below you can find a short routine that you can do before bedtime.

Another great way to feel more relaxed and at ease in your body is by setting aside 5-10 minutes for a meditation. This practice also teaches you how to be here, in the present moement more.

Relaxing activities are really personal to the individual. What may be relaxing for one may not be for another.
